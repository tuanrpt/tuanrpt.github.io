I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [Qatar Computing Research Institute (QCRI)](https://cs.qcri.org/), [Hamad Bin Khalifa University](https://www.hbku.edu.qa/) in Doha, Qatar.

I am an AI researcher with over **8 years of experience** in **Deep Learning**, **Machine Learning**, **Computer Vision**, and **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**. My research has been published and presented at leading AI conferences, including **ICML**, **ICCV**, **ICLR**, **UAI**, and **IJCAI**, and spans a broad range of topics such as **deepfake detection**, **multimodal learning**, **generative AI**, **large language model (LLM) safety**, **domain adaptation**, and **transfer learning**. In addition to my research contributions, I have over **3 years of experience** mentoring **200+ students** at **Monash University**.

Previously, I earned my **PhD in Computer Science** from [Monash University](https://www.monash.edu/), Australia, and my **Bachelor's degree in Computer Science** from [HCMUE](https://hcmue.edu.vn/en/), Vietnam.
